Organize Your Teamwork. Instantly.

NotedTeam is a collaborative to-do list application built with Flutter and Go. Manage tasks, track progress, and stay in sync with your team, anytime, anywhere.

Why NotedTeam?

Structured To-do Lists

Organize your tasks with clear titles, descriptions, and statuses.

Team Collaboration

Easily invite members, manage teams, and see who's responsible for what.

Prioritize with Urgency

Set urgency levels and due dates to focus on what matters most.
